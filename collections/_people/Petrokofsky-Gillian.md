Gill is a research fellow at the University of Oxford. She focuses on research to support evidence-based decision making in forestry and land-use. She works in multidisciplinary teams to engage communities of interest, who collectively contribute to exploring and solving contemporary problems facing the sector. She co-authored the latest <a href="http://www.environmentalevidence.org/information-for-authors">Guidelines and Standards for Evidence Synthesis in Environmental Management</a>.
