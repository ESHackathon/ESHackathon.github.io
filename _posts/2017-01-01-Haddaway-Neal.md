<img align="left" width="150" height="150" src="/assets/people/Haddaway_Neal.jpg" alt="Neal Haddaway"/>Neal is a research fellow at the Stockholm Environment Institute. His main research interests are around the production and use of environmental evidence in decision-making, by improving the transparency, efficiency and reliability of evidence synthesis as a methodology. Neal is the co-creator of ROSES (<a href="http://www.roses-reporting.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">RepOrting standards for Systematic Evidence Syntheses</a>), a set of rigorous standards for reporting the conduct of systematic reviews and maps in environmental topics.
